Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 November 2025

Atenix Messenger-IG ("we", "our", "us") operates an Instagram automation tool that helps respond to user comments and messages via the Instagram API. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information through the Instagram Graph API after you authorize our app:

  • Instagram account ID
  • Username
  • Profile name
  • Comments made on your posts
  • Messages sent to your Instagram inbox
  • Public interactions (likes, mentions, etc.)
  • Data required to send automated responses (such as sending your resource link via DM)

We DO NOT collect:

  • Passwords
  • Sensitive personal data
  • Financial information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data only to:

  • Read comments on your Instagram posts
  • Send automatic replies or resource links via Instagram Direct Messages
  • Notify you of relevant activity
  • Improve our Instagram messaging automation features

We never sell or share your data with third parties.

3. How We Store and Protect Information

We use industry-standard security practices to ensure your data is safe:

  • Secure encrypted servers
  • Restricted internal access
  • No long-term storage of message content unless required for a feature

You may request deletion of your data at any time.

4. Sharing Your Information

We only share data when:

  • Required by law
  • Needed for Instagram API functionality
  • You provide explicit permission

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke app permissions anytime through Instagram settings
  • Opt-out of automated features

6. Third-Party Services

Our service interacts with the following platforms:

  • Meta (Instagram Graph API & Webhooks)
  • Third-party hosting services

Their privacy policies also apply.

7. Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Changes will be posted here with the updated date.
